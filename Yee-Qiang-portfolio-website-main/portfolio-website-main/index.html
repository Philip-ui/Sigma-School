01. About Me

Hello! My name is Chong Kok Onn I grew up in Petaling Jaya. Studied locally in colleges for my tertiary education. Worked in the computer industry most of the time. Firsty in computer printers line, then PC and was promoted to Systems Engineer for Windows Operating Systems. Then I worked in companies as IT Executive maintaining computer hardware and software.

What got me interested in coding was that I was tired of being an IT Executive. So I took some time off to explore other things, I went into Real Estate line but was not my cup of tea. Then something spark in me to try doing website myself and landed into a web development company. I learnt new things but was limited to just building static websites. I could not grow much in that company. However, I thought I could find job security in the company and so I didn't think too much of advancing myself in coding. But things took a turn, the company lost a major client and I was asked to leave. So I started freelancing and my interest in coding grew as I explore it by learning in youtube and free online tutorials but was aimless. After some time finding for an instituition of higher learning, Sigma School suited me best. In that school, my learning and passion for coding grew tremendously as they offer exceptional learning materials and mentoring. Hence, I can learn other computer languages and technologies on my own and build several web projects like Interactive Cocktail Search App and Restaurant website with Take Away Food Ordering feature.
